How do I buy a property through Albeyt Consult?

Buying a property with Albeyt Consult is simple. Browse our listings, filter by your preferences, and contact us for a viewing. Our agents will guide you through negotiations, legal paperwork, and financing options.

How can I rent a property?

To rent a property, explore our rental listings, schedule a viewing, and submit your rental application. We will assist in lease agreements and ensure a smooth move-in process.

What property management services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive property management services, including tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance, and legal compliance. Our goal is to maximize returns for property owners while ensuring tenant satisfaction.

Do you provide project management for real estate developments?

Yes, we manage real estate projects from inception to completion, including budgeting, contractor coordination, quality assurance, and timely project delivery.

Do you offer interior design services?

Yes, Albeyt Consult provides professional interior design services to enhance the aesthetics and functionality of your space. We work with both residential and commercial clients.

What are the legal requirements for buying property in Kenya?

Property buyers in Kenya must conduct due diligence, secure a sales agreement, pay stamp duty, and register the title deed with the Ministry of Lands. Our team will guide you through the entire legal process.

How do I list my property for sale or rent?

You can list your property by contacting us directly. Our agents will assess your property, take professional photos, and market it to potential buyers or tenants.
